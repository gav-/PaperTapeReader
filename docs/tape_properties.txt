Punched tape properties:
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Punched_tape
- 8 hole (8 rows + 1 tractor hole = 8 bits per column/character)
- 0.1 mm thick
- 1 inch wide (25.4 mm)
- Hole spacing 0.1 inch (2.54 mm) in both directions
- Data holes 0.072 inches (1.83 mm) in diameter
- Feed holes 0.046 inches (1.17 mm) in diameter
