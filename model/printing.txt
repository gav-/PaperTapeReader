Printing notes for Paper Tape Reader model:

- rotate base 180 degrees on x-axis (or y-axis), so that the paper path will be
  printed flat on the bed.

- use a 1mm drill bit to ream the feed holes.

- use a 1.5mm drill bit to ream the data holes.

- use a 3mm bit to ream the screw holes.

- it is possible to use a 3mm bit (with drill in reverse) to gently ream
  the LED holes.
